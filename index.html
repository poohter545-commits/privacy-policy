Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Poohter collects, uses, protects, and deletes information for the Poohter Seller app and seller dashboard.

Effective date: May 20, 2026

App covered: Poohter Seller, operated by Poohter.

Contact: support@poohter.com

Information We Collect

When you create or use a seller account, Poohter may collect your name, email address, phone number, password, shop name, business type, city, warehouse address, CNIC number, CNIC images, bank name, account title, account or IBAN number, and mobile wallet number.

We also collect seller activity needed to operate the marketplace, including product details, product images or videos you upload, order records, stock updates, payout records, login/session data, support messages, and basic device or usage information. Product videos may include audio if you choose to upload a video with audio.

How We Use Information

We use seller information to create and secure your account, verify seller identity, review seller applications, operate product listings, process orders, calculate payouts, provide support, prevent fraud or misuse, improve our services, and comply with legal, accounting, tax, and marketplace safety obligations.

Sharing Information

We do not sell seller personal information. We may share information with Poohter administrators and operations teams, hosting and storage providers, email/OTP delivery providers, payment or settlement partners, fraud prevention and security providers, and legal authorities when required by law or needed to protect users and the marketplace.

Security And Retention

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect seller data. We keep account, verification, order, and payout information for as long as needed to provide the service, resolve disputes, meet accounting or legal requirements, prevent fraud, and enforce marketplace policies.

Your Choices And Deletion

You can request deletion of your Poohter Seller account and related personal data from our account deletion page. Some order, payout, tax, security, or fraud-prevention records may be retained where required by law or legitimate business needs.

Children

Poohter Seller is intended for sellers and business operators. It is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the effective date and publish the revised policy on this page.
